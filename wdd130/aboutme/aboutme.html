A Little About Me

I am a father of 4 daughters and a pitbull. My wife and I have been married since 2015, and we have been moving upward slowly, yet surely, ever since. We started our marriage in a small 2-bedroom duplex, and purchased our 3 bedroom home in 2017. I was baptized at the age of 8 to convert parents. I was the first child born under the covenant in my family. I served a mission in the Texas Fort Worth Mission, which is where I returned to after finding my wife in a LDS Singles Facebook Page. My goal is to earn a degree in Applied Technology. My first certificate is going to be in Programming, and I have enjoyed it so much I can't wait to work in the field of coding.
